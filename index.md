I am a restoration ecology scholar studying volunteer participation in ecological restoration projects. I am a researcher in University of Waterloo's [Conservation and Restoration Ecology lab](https://smurphcare.wordpress.com/) lead by [Dr. Stephen D. Murphy](https://uwaterloo.ca/environment-resources-and-sustainability/people-profiles/stephen-murphy). My major areas of research are volunteer participation, community-building and ecological restoration.

You will find a link to [my CV](/CV/) in the menu bar above. There is also [a blog](/blog/), where I write about restoration ecology and the experience of doing a PhD. 

The [Open Restoration](/techniques/) link takes you to a page where I am collecting all of the how-to guides I can find online. This is a project to attempt to democratize restoration knowledge by making it freely available.
